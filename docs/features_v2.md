- Introduction to the author as a writer and computer scientist

- Links to other platforms like GitHub, Goodreads, and Google Scholar

## 9. Design and User Experience
- Minimalist design, easy to read
- Simple navigation with clear links to main sections
- Content-focused with readable formatting
